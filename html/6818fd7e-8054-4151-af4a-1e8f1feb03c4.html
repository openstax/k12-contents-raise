For questions 5–6, divide using synthetic division.

  1. \((b^3-3b^2-4b+12) \div (b+2)\)

    • \(b^2+5b-6\); remainder \(0\)

ANSWER: \( b^2-5b+6\); remainder \(0\)

  1. \((3x^4-11x^3+2x^2+10x+6) \div (x-3)\)
