  1. In Family A, the youngest child is 7 years younger than the oldest, who is 18.

Enter your answer here:

  • \(y=18-7\), for \(y\), the age of the youngest child.
  • \(y+7=18\)
  • \(18−y=7\)
  • \(18−7=11\), where 11 is the age of the youngest child.
  • \(11+7=18\)
  • \(18−11=7\)
