Warm Up Activity


Here is a table of values. The two quantities, \( x \) and \(y \), are related.

\( x \) \( y \)
\(1\) \(0\)
\(3\) \(8\)
\(5\) \(24\)
\(7\) \(48\)

What are some strategies you could use to find a relationship between \( x \) and \(y \)? Brainstorm as many ways as possible.

Enter your answer here:

Your answer may vary, but here are some samples

  • See if there is an operation that could be done to \( x \) that would produce \( y \).
  • Compare how the \( x \)-values change and the \( y \)-values change, and see if there is a pattern.
  • See if the numbers in one column follow a special pattern and if that pattern could be connected to the numbers in the other column.
  • Plot the \( x \)- and \( y \)-values on a coordinate plane and see if the graph looks like a familiar relationship.
