Evaluate each expression for \(a=9\), \(b=-5\), and \(c=-2\).

  1. \(-b\)

Enter the value of \(-b\).

Correct! \(-(-5)=5\)

The correct answer is 5: \(-(-5)=5\).

  1. \(b^2\)

Enter the value of \(b^2\).

Correct! \((-5)^2=25\)

The correct answer is 25: \((-5)^2=25\).

  1. \(b^2−4ac\)

Enter the value of \(b^2−4ac\).

Correct! \((-5)^2−4(9)(-2)=25-(36)(-2)=25-(-72)=25+72=97\)

The correct answer is 97: \((-5)^2−4(9)(-2)=25-(36)(-2)=25-(-72)=25+72=97\).
