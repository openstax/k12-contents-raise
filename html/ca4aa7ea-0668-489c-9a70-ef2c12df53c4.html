Student Activity

Find all solutions to \((x+5)(2x−3)=0\). Explain or show your reasoning.

Answer: \(x=-5\) and \(x=\frac {3}{2}\)

By the zero product property, either \(x+5=0\) or \(2x−3=0\), so \(x=-5\) and \(x=\frac {3}{2}\).
