Warm Up Activity


Here is a riddle: “I am thinking of two numbers that add up to 5.678. The difference between them is 9.876. What are the two numbers?”

  1. Name any pair of numbers whose sum is 5.678.
  2. Name any pair of numbers whose difference is 9.876.
  3. The riddle can be represented with two equations. Write the equations.
  4. Solve the riddle. Explain your reasoning.


Enter your answers here:

Compare your answers:

  1. For example: 2 and 3.678.
  2. For example: 10 and 0.124.
  3. Let \(x\) and \(y\) represent the numbers: \(x+y=5.678\) and \(x-y=9.876\).
  4. The two numbers are \(7.777\) and \(-2.099\). For example:
  • Graphing the two equations gives two lines that intersect at \((7.777,-2.099)\).

  • Adding the two equations eliminates the \(y\)-variable and gives \(2x=15.554\) or \(x=7.777\). Substituting this \(x\)-value into either equation gives \(y=-2.099\).
