Student Activity

Write each expression in factored form. If it is not possible, write “not possible.”

  1. \(a^2− 36\)

Answer: \((a + 6)(a − 6)\)

  1. \(49 − 25b^2\)

Answer: \((7 + 5b)(7 − 5b)\)

  1. \(c^2+ 9\)

Answer: Not possible because this is the sum of two squares.

  1. \(\frac {100}{81}-16d^2\)

Answer: \((\frac {10}{9} + 4d)(\frac {10}{9} − 4d)\)
