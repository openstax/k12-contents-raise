Step 1 - \(x^2+3x=-8\)

Step 2 - \(4x^2+4(3x)=4(-8)\)

Step 3 - \((2x)^2+6(2x)=-32\)

Step 4 - \(P^2+6P=-32\)

Step 5 - \(P^2+6P+3^2=-32+3^2\)

Step 6 - \((P+3)^2=3^2-32\)

Step 7 - \(P+3= \pm \sqrt{3^2-32}\)

Step 8 - \(P=-3 \pm \sqrt{3^2-32}\)

Step 9 - \(2x=-3 \pm \sqrt{3^2-32}\)

Step 10
\(x= \frac{-3 \pm \sqrt{3^2-32}}{2}\)
