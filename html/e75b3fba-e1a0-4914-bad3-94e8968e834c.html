The equation of a line of best fit is \(y = 9.875 + 0.02039x\), where \(x\) represents the gestational time, in days, and \(y\) represents longevity, in years. 


A lion’s gestation time is 100 days, and its longevity is 15 years. What does this line of best fit predict the lion’s longevity to be?


Solution

The line of best fit is \(ùë¶=9.875 + 0.02039(100)=11.9\)

The residual would be: actual value – estimated value or \(15‚àí11.9=3.1\).

The residual value is 3.1.
