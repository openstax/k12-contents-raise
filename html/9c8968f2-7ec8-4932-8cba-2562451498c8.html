In this lesson, you will develop two different methods to factor special polynomials. While these polynomials can be factored using the methods from the previous lesson, these special cases allow for a more streamlined approach to factoring. One important skill that you will develop is the ability to recognize when you have a polynomial that fits into these special cases.

When you finish this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Factor perfect square trinomials.
  • Factor differences of squares.
