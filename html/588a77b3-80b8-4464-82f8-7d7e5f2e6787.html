  2. What is the value of \(P(9.1)\)? What does it tell us about the side length and perimeter of the square?

    3. ANSWER: \(P(9.1)=36.4\). It tells us that when the side length of the square is 9.1 inches, the perimeter of the square is 36.4 inches.
