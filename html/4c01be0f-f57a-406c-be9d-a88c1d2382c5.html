  1. \(g=180−\frac{1}{3}t\)
  2. \(g=180 \cdot (\frac{1}{3})^t\)
  3. \(g=\frac{1}{3} \cdot 180^t\)
  4. \(g=180 \cdot (\frac{2}{3})^t\)
