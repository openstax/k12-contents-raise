In this lesson, you will write a system of equations from a table of values, graphs, and situations. You will also be able to graph systems by hand and use graphs of systems of equations to estimate real world solutions.

When you finish this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Write a system of equations from tables of values and graphs.
  • Graph systems of equations by hand.
  • Estimate real-world solutions by graphing systems of equations.
