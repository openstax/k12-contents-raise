Here are pairs of equivalent expressions in standard form and factored form. Find the missing symbols and numbers.

  1. \(x^2 \;\square\; 16x \;\square\; 17\) and \((x + 1)(x − 17)\)

Answer: − and –. The completed expression is \(x^2− 16x − 17\).

  1. \(x^2 \;\square\; 16x\; \square\; 17\) and \((x − 1)(x + 17)\)

Answer: + and −, respectively. The completed expression is \(x^2+16x −17\).

  1. \(x^2+3x −28\) and \((x + \square)(x − \square)\)

Answer: 7 and 4, respectively. The completed expression is \((x + 7)(x − 4)\).

  1. \(x^2−12x −28\) and \((x \;\square \;\square)(x \;\square \;\square)\)

Answer: −14 and +2. The completed expression is \((x − 14)(x + 2)\).
