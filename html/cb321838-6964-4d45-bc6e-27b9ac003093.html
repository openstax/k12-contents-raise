Cool Down (5 minutes)

Student Activity

Diego’s club earns money for charity when members of the club perform community service after school. For each student who does community service, the club earns $5. There are 12 students in the club.

The total dollar amount earned, \(E\), is a function of the number of members who perform community service, \(n\).

  1. Is 5 a possible input value? Why or why not?

    2. ANSWER: \(n=5\) is possible. It means that 5 students perform community service.

  2. Is 24 a possible output value? Why or why not?

    3. ANSWER: No, 24 is not possible as an output value. An output value has to be a multiple of 5.

  3. Describe the domain of this function.

    4. ANSWER: All integers from 0 to 12

  4. Describe the range of this function.

    5. ANSWER: Multiples of 5 from 0 to 60
