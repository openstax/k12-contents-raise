Cool Down Activity

Consider the equation \( 1.5x + 4.5y = 18 \). 

For each question, explain or show your reasoning.

1. If we graph the equation, what is the slope of the graph?

The slope is \( -\frac{1}{3} \). Rearranging the equation to isolate \( y \) gives \( y=-\frac{1}{3}x\ +\ 4 \).


2. Where does the graph intersect the \( y \)-axis?

The graph intersects the y-axis at \( (0, 4) \). The \( 4 \) in \( y=-\frac{1}{3}x+4 \) tells us where the graph crosses the \( y \)-axis.


3. Where does it intersect the \( x \)-axis?

The slope is \( 1-\frac{1}{3} \).  Rearranging the equation to isolate y gives \( 1y=-\frac{1}{3}x\ +\ 4 \).
