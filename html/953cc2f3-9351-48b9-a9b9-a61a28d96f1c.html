In this lesson, you will multiply different types of polynomials. You will learn methods to multiply binomials by other binomials. You will learn different methods to multiply polynomials by other polynomials, including using polynomial functions.

Polynomials are frequently used in calculating physics problems and other real-world situations involving projectiles and the motion of objects. Mastering the polynomial operations in this unit will help you in future studies.

When you finish this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Multiply monomials.
  • Multiply a polynomial by a monomial.
  • Multiply binomials.
  • Multiply a polynomial by a polynomial.
  • Multiply special products.
  • Multiply polynomial functions.
