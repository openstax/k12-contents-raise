Cool Down (5 minutes)

Student Activity

Draw a tic-tac-toe board on an extra sheet of paper. Use the polynomial functions shown here for the activity.

\(f(x) = 8x^2-6x-2\)
\(h(x) = 6x^2-4x-1\)

For each polynomial problem below, place an \(X\) on your tic-tac-toe board corresponding to the location of its matching solution.

\(16x^2-9x^2 =\) ________
\((9x^2-5x-3)+(2x^2+8x-3) =\) ________
\(f(5) - h(-2) =\) ________
\((7x^2-3x+7)-(3x^2+6x-4) =\) ________
\(f(4) =\) ________

For each polynomial problem below, place an \(O\) on your tic-tac-toe board corresponding to the location of its matching solution.

\(11x^3+12x^3 =\) ________
\(f(x) - h(x) =\) ________
\(f(x) + h(x) =\) ________
\(h(-3) =\) ________

Did the \(Xs\) or \(Os\) win the tic-tac-toe game?

Answer: The \(Xs\) won the game.

Xs:

\(16x^2-9x^2 = 7x^2\)
\((9x^2-5x-3)+(2x^2+8x-3) = 11x^2+3x-6\)
\(f(5) - h(-2) = 137\)
\(f(4) = 102\)
\((7x^2-3x+7)-(3x^2+6x-4) = 4x^2-9x+11\)

Os:

\(11x^3+12x^3 = 23x^3\)
\(f(x) - h(x) = 2x^2-2x-1\)
\(f(x) + h(x) = 14x^2-10x-3\)
\(h(-3) = 65\)
