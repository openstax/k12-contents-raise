In this unit, students will:

  • Identify quadratic functions from expressions, tables, and graphs.
  • Represent quadratic equations in standard and factored form.
  • Graph quadratic equations from standard form, factored form, and vertex form.
  • Describe how the \(a\), \(b\), and \(c\) in \(ax^2 + bx + c\) affect the graph of a quadratic equation.
  • Determine whether a visual pattern represents a linear, exponential, or quadratic relationship.

To accomplish these unit-level learning goals, students will complete the following lessons and activities.
