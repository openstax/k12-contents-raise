Compare your Answer:

  • \(b\) is -4, so in Step 4, the value \(-b\) should be -(-4), which is 4.
  • In Step 1, the equation should be rewritten as \(ax^2+bx+c=0\), so the constant term \(c\) is -20, not 20. So, in Step 4, the last part of the numerator should be \(\sqrt{(-4)^2-4(3)(-20)}\).
  • In Step 4, \((-4)^2\) should be 16. Squaring a negative number gives a positive number.
