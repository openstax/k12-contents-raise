Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

  1. Which expression is equal to \(4^0\cdot 4^2\)?
    1. 0
    2. 1
    3. 16
    4. 64

ANSWER: c. 16

  1. Select three expressions that are equivalent to \(3^8\).

    1. \(8^3\)
    2. \(\frac{3^{10}}{3^2}\)
    3. \(3\cdot 8\)
    4. \(\left(3^4\right)^2\)
    5. \(\left(3\cdot3\right)^4\)
    6. \(\frac1{3^8}\)

ANSWER: b.\(\frac{3^{10}}{3^2}\), d. \(\left(3^4\right)^2\), e. \(\left(3\cdot3\right)^4\)

  1. An investment bond is initially bought for $250. It doubles in value every decade. Choose an equation that relates \(V\), the value of the bond, to \(d\), the number of decades since the bond was bought.
    1. \(V = 250\cdot d^2\)
    2. \(V = 500^d\)
    3. \(V = 250\cdot 2\cdot d\)
    4. \(V = 250\cdot 2^d\)

ANSWER: d. \(V = 250\cdot 2^d\)

  1. A sea turtle population \(p\) is modeled by the equation \(p=400\cdot(1.25)^y\) where \(y\) is the number of years since the population was first measured. How many turtles are in the population when it is first measured?

ANSWER: 400
