3. \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}2x+y=-2\\3x-y=7\end{array}\right.\)

Correct! (1,−4)

Try again. Revisit activities 2.2.3 and 2.2.4.

The correct answer is \((1,−4)\).

Check the solution in both equations.

\(\begin{array}{rcl}2x+y&=&-2 \\ 2(1)+(-4)&=&-2 \\ 2-4&=&-2 \\ -2&=&-2\checkmark \\ \end{array}\)


\(\begin{array}{rcl}3x-y&=&7 \\ 3(1)-(-4)&=&7 \\ 3+4&=&7 \\ 7&=&7\checkmark \\ \end{array}\)

(4,-1) Makes both equations in the system true, therefore it is a solution.
