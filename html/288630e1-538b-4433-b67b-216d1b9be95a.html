Lesson Narrative

This lesson reinforces the concept of the solution of a system of two linear equations. Students are also asked to write systems of equations from a table of values and connect that to a graph of a system of equations using technology.

Students then write the systems of equations from a graph and solve the system using the graph. In the cool down, students will explore how a system of equations represents a real world problem.

Making internet-enabled devices available gives students an opportunity to choose appropriate tools strategically.

Learning Goals (Teacher Facing)

Students will be able to:

  • Write a system of equations from a table of values, a graph, and a verbal description.
  • Graph systems of equations by hand and determine solutions.
  • Estimate solutions to real-world problems by graphing systems of equations.

Learning Targets (Student Facing)

  • Write a system of equations.
  • Graph systems of equations by hand.
  • Estimate real-world solutions by graphing systems of equations.

Lesson Vocabulary

During this lesson, it is important to:
