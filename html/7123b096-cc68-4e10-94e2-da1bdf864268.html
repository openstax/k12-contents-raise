  1. Solve each equation by completing the square.
  1. \(25x^2+40x=-12\)

Write down your answer, then select the solution button to compare your work.

Compare your answer:

\(\begin{aligned} 25 x^{2}+40 x &=-12 \\ 25 x^{2}+40 x+12 &=0 \\ 25 x^{2}+40 x+12+4 &=4 \\ 25 x^{2}+40 x+16 &=4 \\ (5 x+4)^{2} &=4 \\ \sqrt{(5 x+4)^{2}} &=\sqrt{4} \\ 5 x+4 &=2 \\ 5 x+4 &=-2 \\ 5 x &=-2 \\ 5 x &=-6 \\ \\ x=-\frac{2}{5} & \text { and } x=-\frac{6}{5} \end{aligned} \)
