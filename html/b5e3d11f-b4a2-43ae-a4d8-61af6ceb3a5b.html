Solve each system without graphing.

  1. \( \begin{cases} 5x - 2y = 26 \\ y + 4 = x \end{cases} \)

  2. \( \begin{cases} 2m - 2p = -6\\ p = 2m + 10 \end{cases} \)

  3. \( \begin{cases} 2d = 8f \\ 18 - 4f = 2d \end{cases} \)

  4. \( \begin{cases} w + \frac17z = 4 \\ z = 3w -2 \end{cases} \)

Enter your answer here:

There are many ways to solve these equations, graphing and algebraic solving, guessing and checking, or using tables. Answers are:

  1. \( x = 6, y = 2 \)
  2. \( m = -7, p = -4 \)
  3. \( d = 6, f = \frac32 \)
  4. \( w = 3, z = 7 \)
