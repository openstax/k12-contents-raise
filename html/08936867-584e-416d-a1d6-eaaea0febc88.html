Han and Jada solved the same quadratic equation with different methods. Here they are:

Han's Method Jada's Method

\(\begin{aligned}(x-6)^{2} &=25 \\ (x-6)(x-6) &=25 \\ x^{2}-12 x+36 &=25 \\ x^{2}-12 x+11 &=0 \\ (x-11)(x-1) &=0 \\ (x-11) &=0 \\ (x-1) &=0 \\ x=11 \text { or } x &=1 \end{aligned}\)

\(\begin{aligned}(x-6)^{2} &=25 \\ \sqrt{(x-6)^{2}} &= \sqrt{25} \\ x-6 &=\pm 5 \\ x-6 &=5 \\ x-6 &=-5 \\ x=11 \text { or } x &=1\end{aligned}\)
