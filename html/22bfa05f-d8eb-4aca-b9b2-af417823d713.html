Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

  1. Evaluate (\frac{140-20}{9-12}\) mentally.

Answer: -40

\(\frac{140-20}{9-12}=\frac{120-3}=-40\)

  1. Find the slope of the line that goes through the points \((1,-3)\) and \((2,4)\).:

Answer: 7

\(m=\frac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1}=\frac{4-(-3)}{2-1}=71=7\)

  1. Write the equation of the line in slope-intercept form that goes through \((0,3)\) and has a slope of \(\frac14\) .

  1. Select two equations that are correctly in standard form.
  • \(6x - 3y = 3 \)
  • \( -4x + 5y = 9 \)
  • \( \frac{1}{2}x + 3y = 8 \)
  • \( 5x + 2y = 9\)
  • \( 5y + 2x = 10 \)

Answer: \(6x - 3y = 3\) and \(5x + 2y = 9\)

\(6x - 3y = 3\) and \(5x + 2y = 9\) are in standard form because they are of the form \(Ax + By = C\).

They do not have any fractions or decimals and start with a positive coefficient.

  1. Write \(y = 4x - 2\) in standard form.

Answer: \(4x -y = 2\)

\(\begin{array}{rcl}y &=& 4x - 2\\ y - 4x &=& 4x - 4x - 2\\ -4x + y &=& -2 \\-1(-4x + y &=& -2) \\4x - y &=& 2\end{array}\)

  1. Write the equation \(y-4=-2(x-5)\) in slope-intercept form.

Answer: \(y=-2x+14\)

\(\begin{array}{rcl}y-4&=&-2(x-5) \\ y-4&=&-2x+10\\ y&=&-2x+10+4\\ y&=&-2x+14\end{array}\)

  1. Find the slope of the line between the points \((3,-1)\) and \((2,-4)\).

\(m=\frac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1}=\frac{-4-(-1)}{2-3}=\frac{-3}{-1}=3\)

  1. Write the equation \(y-2=3(x+5)\) in standard form.

Answer: \(3x-y=-17\)

\(\begin{array}{rcl}y-2&=&3(x+5)\\y-2&=&3x+15\\y-2-3x&=&3x-3x+15\\y-3x-2&=&15\\y-3x&=&15+2\\y-3x&=&17\\-1-3x+y&=&17\\3x-y&=&-17\end{array}\)
