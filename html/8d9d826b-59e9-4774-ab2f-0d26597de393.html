Solve questions 11-12 by graphing.

11. A bakery charges $2 per croissant and $4 per muffin. One day, they charge $42 in total and sell 15 baked goods. How many croissants and muffins did they sell?

  1. 5 croissants and 10 muffins
  2. 9 croissants and 6 muffins
  3. 10 croissants and 5 muffins
  4. 8 croissants and 7 muffins

Answer:9 croissants and 6 muffins

12. A pet store sells mice for $7 each and gerbils for $9 each. They sell 17 animals for a total of $137. How many mice and gerbils do they sell?

  1. 15 mice and 2 gerbils
  2. 8 mice and 9 gerbils
  3. 7 mice and 10 gerbils
  4. 6 mice and 11 gerbils

Answer:8 mice and 9 gerbils
