Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

  1. The number of people with the flu during an epidemic is a function, \(f\), of the number of days, \(d\), since the epidemic began. The equation \(f(d)=50 \cdot (\frac{3}{2})^d\) defines \(f\).
    1. How many people had the flu at the beginning of the epidemic? Do not enter commas or spaces in your answer.

ANSWER: 50

    1. What does \(f(1)\) mean in this situation? 

ANSWER: The number of people infected 1 day after the epidemic began.

2. The function, \(f\), gives the dollar value of a bond \(t\) years after the bond was purchased. The graph of \(f\) is shown.

    1. What is \(f(0)\)? Do not enter commas or spaces in your answer.

ANSWER: 1000

    1. When is \(f(t)=1500\)? Estimate the nearest whole number. Insert integers only.

ANSWER: 8

  1. A function \(f\) gives the number of stray cats in a town \(t\) years since the town started an animal control program. The program includes both sterilizing stray cats and finding homes to adopt them. An equation representing \(f\) is \(f(t)=243(\frac{1}{3})^t\).
    1. What is the value of \(f(t)\) when \(t\) is 0?

ANSWER: 243

    1. What is the value of \(f(t)\) when \(t\) is 1?

ANSWER: 81

  1. Function \(g\) gives the amount of a chemical in a person’s body, in milligrams, \(t\) hours since the patient took the drug. The equation \(g(t)=600 \cdot (\frac{3}{5})^t\) defines this function.
    1. Which is the best choice for the domain of \(g\)?
      1. \(t \geq 0\)
      2. \(t \geq 600\)
      3. \(t \leq 600\)
      4. \(0 \leq t \leq 600\)

ANSWER: a. \(t \geq 0\)

    1. Which is the best choice for the range of \(g\)?
      1. \(g>0\)
      2. \(g \geq 600\)
      3. \(g \leq 600\)
      4. \(0<g \leq 600\)

ANSWER: d.  \(0<g \leq 600\)
