Cool Down Activity

The perimeter of a rectangle is 48 centimeters. The relationship between the length, the width, and the perimeter of the rectangle can be described with the equation: \( 2 \cdot \text{length} +2 \cdot \text{width}=48 \).

Find the length, in centimeters, if the width is:

  1. 10 centimeters

Enter your answer here.

Correct! 14

\(2L + 2(10) = 48\)
\(2L + 20 = 48\)
\(2L + 20 - 20 = 48 - 20\)
\(2L = 28\)
\(\frac{1}{2} (2L) =\frac{1}{2} (28)\)
\(L = 14\)
