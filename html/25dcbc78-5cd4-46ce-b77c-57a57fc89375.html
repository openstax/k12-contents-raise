For questions 9-10, add the equations in the following systems of equations. What is the resulting new equation?

  1. \(\begin{array}{c}\left\{\begin{array}{l}4x\;-\;6y\;=\;14\\-4x\;+\;y\;=\;-19\end{array}\right.\\\\\end{array}\)
  • \(-5y = -5\)
  • \(-5x = -5\)

  • \(-7y = -5\)

  1. \(\begin{array}{c}\left\{\begin{array}{l}3x\;-\;5y\;=\;18\\7x\;+\;5y\;=\;-8\end{array}\right.\\\\\end{array}\)
