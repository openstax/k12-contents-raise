Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

For questions 1-8, solve each system of equations to determine if there is one solution, no solution, or infinitely many solutions.

  1. \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}5x\;-\;3y\;&=&\;7\\x\;-\;0.6y\;&=&\;1.4\end{array}\right.\)

Answer: infinitely many solutions

  1. \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}-x+2y&=&-1\\\;5x-10y&amp;=6\end{array}\right.\)

Answer: no solution

  1. \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}7x+6y&=&2\\-28x-24y&=&-8\end{array}\right.\)

Answer: infinitely many solutions

  1. \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}-0.2x\;+\;0.4y\;&=&\;0.6\\x-2y&=&-3\end{array}\right.\)

Answer: infinitely many solutions

  1. \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}-0.1x\;+\;0.2y\;&=&\;0.6\\5x-10y&=&1\end{array}\right.\)

Answer: no solution

  1. \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}3x+6y&=&11\\\;2x+4y&=&9\end{array}\right.\)

Answer: no solution

  1. \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}-x+2y=4\\\;2x-4y&=&1\end{array}\right.\)

Answer: no solution

  1. \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}3x-2y&=&5\\\;-9x+6y&=&-15\end{array}\right.\)

Answer: infinitely many solutions

  1. Which system of equations has infinitely many solutions?
  • \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}4x\;+\;3y\;&=&\;12\\\;8x\;+\;6y\;&=&\;-24\end{array}\right.\)

  • \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}4x\;+\;3y\;&=&\;12\\\;8x\;+\;6y\;&=&\;12\end{array}\right.\)

  • \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}-4x\;+\;3y\;&=&\;12\\\;8x\;-\;6y\;&=&\;24\end{array}\right.\)

  • \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}-4x\;+\;3y\;&=&\;-12\\-8x\;+\;6y\;&=&\;-24\end{array}\right.\)

Answer: \(-4x + 3y = -12\) and \( -8x + 6y = -24 \)

  1. Which system of equations has no solution?

  • \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}3x\;+\;7y\;&=&\;15\\3x\;+\;7y\;&=&\;40\end{array}\right.\)

  • \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}3x\;+\;7y\;&=&\;15\\6x\;+\;14y\;&=&\;30\end{array}\right.\)

  • \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}3x\;-\;7y\;&=&\;15\\\;3x\;+\;7y\;&=&\;40\end{array}\right.\)

  • \(\left\{\begin{array}{rcl}9x\;+\;21y\;&=&\;270\\3x\;+\;7y\;&=&\;90\end{array}\right.\)

Answer: \( 3x + 7y = 15 \) and \( 3x + 7y = 40 \)
