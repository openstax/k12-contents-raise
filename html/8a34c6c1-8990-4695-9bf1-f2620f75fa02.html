Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

For questions 1-8, solve each system of equations to determine if there is one solution, no solution, or infinitely many solutions.

1.  \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}5x\;-\;3y\;=\;7\\x\;-\;0.6y\;=\;1.4\end{array}\right.\)

ANSWER: infinitely many solutions

2. \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}-x+2y=-1\\\;5x-10y=6\end{array}\right.\)

ANSWER: no solution

3.  \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}7x+6y=2\\-28x-24y=-8\end{array}\right.\)

ANSWER: infinitely many solutions

4.  \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}-0.2x\;+\;0.4y\;=\;0.6\\x-2y=-3\end{array}\right.\)

ANSWER: infinitely many solutions

5.  \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}-0.1x\;+\;0.2y\;=\;0.6\\5x-10y=1\end{array}\right.\)

ANSWER: no solution

6.  \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}3x+6y=11\\\;2x+4y=9\end{array}\right.\)
ANSWER: no solution

7.  \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}-x+2y=4\\\;2x-4y=1\end{array}\right.\)

ANSWER: no solution

8.  \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}3x-2y=5\\\;-9x+6y=-15\end{array}\right.\)

ANSWER: infinitely many solutions

9. Which system of equations has infinitely many solutions?

a.
\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}4x\;+\;3y\;=\;12\\\;8x\;+\;6y\;=\;24\end{array}\right.\)
b.
\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}4x\;+\;3y\;=\;12\\\;8x\;+\;6y\;=\;12\end{array}\right.\)
c.
\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}-4x\;+\;3y\;=\;12\\\;8x\;-\;6y\;=\;12\end{array}\right.\)
d.
\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}-4x\;+\;3y\;=\;-12\\-8x\;+\;6y\;=\;-24\end{array}\right.\)

ANSWER:  \(-4x + 3y = 12\)  and \( -8x + 6y = 24 \)

10. Which system of equations has no solution?

a.
\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}3x\;+\;7y\;=\;15\\3x\;+\;7y\;=\;40\end{array}\right.\)
b.
\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}3x\;+\;7y\;=\;15\\6x\;+\;14y\;=\;30\end{array}\right.\)
c.
\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}3x\;-\;7y\;=\;15\\\;3x\;+\;7y\;=\;40\end{array}\right.\)
d.
\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}9x\;+\;21y\;=\;270\\3x\;+\;7y\;=\;90\end{array}\right.\)

ANSWER: \( 3x + 7y = 15 \) and \( 3x + 7y = 40 \)
