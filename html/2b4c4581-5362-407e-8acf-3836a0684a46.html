Student Activity

Here is an equation: \( 5x-2y=10 \).

Create a second equation that would make a system of equations with:

  1. One solution

Answer: Compare Your Answer. Your answer may vary, but here are some samples.

\(3x - y = 7\)
\(2x - 5y = 10\)

  1. No solutions

Answer: Compare Your Answer. Your answer may vary, but here are some samples.

\(5x - 2y = 20\)
\(5x - 2y = 8\)

  1. Infinitely many solutions

Answer: Compare Your Answer. Your answer may vary, but here are some samples.

\(15x - 6y = 30\)
\(150x - 20y = 100\)
