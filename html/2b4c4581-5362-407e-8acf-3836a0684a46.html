Student Activity

Here is an equation: \( 5x-2y=10 \).

Create a second equation that would make a system of equations with:

  1. One solution

Answer: \(3x−y=7 \)

  1. No solutions

Answer: \(5x−2y=20 \)

  1. Infinitely many solutions

Answer: \( 15x−6y=30\)
