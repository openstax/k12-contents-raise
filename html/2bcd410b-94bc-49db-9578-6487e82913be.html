Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

  1. Alan is loading a pallet with boxes that each weigh 45 pounds. The pallet can safely support no more than 900 pounds. How many boxes can he safely load onto the pallet?

____ boxes or less

Answer: 20

  1. The elevator in Yehire’s apartment building has a sign that says the maximum weight is 2,100 pounds. If the average weight of one person is 150 pounds, how many people can safely ride the elevator?

a maximum of ___ people

Answer: 14

  1. Andre is looking at apartments with three of his friends. They want the monthly rent to be no more than $2,360. If the roommates split the rent evenly among the four of them, what is the maximum rent each will pay?

$____

Answer: 590

  1. Arleen got a $20 gift card for the coffee shop. Her favorite iced drink costs $3.79. What is the maximum number of drinks she can buy with the gift card?

____ drinks

Answer: 5

  1. Teegan likes to play golf. He has budgeted $60 next month for the driving range. It costs him $10.55 for a bucket of balls each time he goes. What is the maximum number of times he can go to the driving range next month?

Answer: 5

  1. Ryan charges his neighbors $17.50 to wash their car. How many cars must he wash next summer if his goal is to earn at least $1,500?

____ cars

Answer: 86

  1. Jake’s water bill is $24.80 per month plus $2.20 per ccf (hundred cubic feet) of water. What is the maximum number of ccf Jake can use if he wants his bill to be no more than $60?

____ ccf


Answer: 16

  1. Look at the inequality \(5m + 6 < 14 - 2m\). Which of the following is NOT true?

    1. If \(m\) is a negative number, then \(5m + 6\) could be a positive or negative number.
    2. If \(m\) is a negative number, then \(14 - 2m\) will always be positive.
    3. If \(m\) is a positive number, then \(5m + 6\) will always be positive.
    4. If \(m\) is a negative number, then \(14 - 2m\) will always be negative.


Answer: If m is a negative number, then 14 − 2m will always be negative
