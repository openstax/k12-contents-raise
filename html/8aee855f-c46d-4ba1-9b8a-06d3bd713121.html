Reinforcing the Substitution Method

Solve the system by substitution:

\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}-4x\;+\;2y\;=\;4\\6x\;+\;-y\;=\;8\end{array}\right.\)

We need to solve one equation for one variable. We will solve the first equation for \(y\).

Step 1 - Solve the first equation for \(y\).

\(\begin{array}{rcl} 4x+2y&=&4 \\2y&=&-4x+4 \\y&=&-2x+2 \\ \end{array}\)

Step 2 - Substitute \(−2x+2\) for \(y\) in the second equation.

\(\begin{array}{rcl} 6x-y&=&8 \\6x-(-2x+2)&=&8 \\ \end{array}\)

Step 3 - Solve the equation for \(x\).

\(\begin{array}{rcl} 6x-(-2x+2)&=&8 \\6x+2x-2&=&8 \\8x-2&=&8 \\8x&=&10 \\x&=&\frac{10}{8} \\x&=&\frac54 \\ \end{array}\)

Step 4 - Substitute \(x=\frac54\) into \(4x+2y=4\) to find \(y\).

\(\begin{array}{rcl} 4x+2y&=&4 \\ 4(\frac54)+2y&=&4 \\5+2y&=&4 \\2y&=&-1 \\y&=&\frac{-1}{2} \\ \end{array}\)

Step 5 - Check the ordered pair. \((\frac54,\frac{-1}{2})\) in both equations.

\(\begin{array}{rr}   & 6x-y=8\\   & 6(\frac54)-(-\frac12)\overset?=8\\ 4x+2y=4 & \frac{30}{4}-(-\frac12)\overset?=8\\ 4(-\frac12)+2(\frac54)\overset?=4 & \frac{15}{2}-(-\frac12)\overset?=8\\ 5-1\overset?=4 & \frac{15}{2}+\frac12\overset?=8 \\ 4=4\checkmark & 8=8\checkmark\\ \end{array}\)
