Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

For questions 1 - 2, determine the number of solutions. Then solve the equation.

  1. \(3x^2=48\)
  • 0 solutions
  • 1 solution; \(x=4\)
  • 2 solutions; \(x=4\) and \(x=-4\)
  • 2 solutions; \(x=12\) and \(x=-12\)

Answer: 2 solutions; \(x=4\) and \(x=-4\)

  1. \((x+2)(x+2)=0\)
  • 0 solutions
  • 1 solution; \(x=-2\)
  • 1 solution; \(x=2\)
  • 2 solutions; \(x=2\) and \(x=-2\)

Answer: 1 solution; \(x=-2\)
