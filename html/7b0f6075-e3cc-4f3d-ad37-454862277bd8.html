Writing Functions from Graphs

Find the equation of the function graphed above. Then find the value at \(f(5)\).

  1. Find the value of \(a\), the starting point.
\(a=4\)
  1. Find the \(b\), the growth factor.
\(b=3\)
  1. Write the equation of the function.
\(f(x)=4 \cdot 3^x\)
  1. Find \(f(5)\).
\(f(5)=4 \cdot 3^5=972\)
