Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

  1. A line of best fit for a data set is \(y=1.1x+3.4\). Find the residual for each of the coordinate pairs, \((x,y)\).
    1. \((5,8.8)\)
      ANSWER: \(-0.1\)
    2. \((2.5,5.95)\)
      ANSWER: \(-0.2\)
    3. \((0,3.72)\)
      ANSWER: \(0.32\)
    4. \((1.5,5.05)\)
      ANSWER: \(0\)
    5. \((-3,0)\)
      ANSWER: \(-0.1\)
    6. \((-5,-4.86)\)
      ANSWER: \(-2.76\)
  1. In the scatter plot below, the length of fish and the age of the fish are shown. The residual for the fish that is 2 years old and 100 mm long is 8.06 mm.


What does this residual mean?

ANSWER: The actual fish at 2 years old is about 8 mm longer than the linear model estimates.
