  1. \((5,8.8)\)
    Answer: \(-0.1\)

  2. \((2.5,5.95)\)
    Answer: \(-0.2\)

  3. \((0,3.72)\)
    Answer: \(0.32\)

  4. \((1.5,5.05)\)
    Answer: \(0\)

  5. \((-3,0)\)
    Answer: \(-0.1\)

  6. \((-5,-4.86)\)

  7. In the scatter plot below, the length of fish and the age of the fish are shown. The residual for the fish that is 2 years old and 100 mm long is 8.06 mm.


What does this residual mean?

Answer: The actual fish at 2 years old is about 8 mm longer than the linear model estimates.
