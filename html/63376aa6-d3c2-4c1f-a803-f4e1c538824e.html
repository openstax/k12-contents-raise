  1. Translate to an equation: Which equation means nine more than x is equal to 52?
  • \(x+9=52\)
  • \(9x=52\)
  • \(9-x=52\)
  • \(\frac{\;52}x=9\)

Answer: \(x + 9 = 52\)

Nine more than \(x\) means that 9 is added to \(x\).
\(x+9\)
Equal to 52 means that we take whatever expression that is in front of 52 and set them equal to each other.
\(x+9=52\)

  1. Translate to an equation: Which equation means ten less than \(m\) is −14?
  • \(10-m=-14\)
  • \(m-10=14\)
  • \(m-10=-14\)
  • \(-14-m=10\)

Answer: \( m - 10 = -14\)

Ten less than \(m\) means that 10 is subtracted from \(m\).
\(m-10\)
The word is, is the same as “equal to”. This means we take whatever expression that is in front of -14 and set them equal to each other.
\(m-10=-14\)

  1. Translate to an equation: Which equation means the sum of \(y\) and −30 is 40?
  • \(-30y=40\)
  • \(y= 40+-30\)
  • \(y+40=-30\)
  • \(y+(-30)=40\)

Answer: \( y + (-30) = 40\)

The sum of \(y\) and -30 means that you add them together.
\(y+(-30)\)
The word is, is the same as “equal to”. This means we take whatever expression that is in front of 40 and set them equal to each other. \(y+(-30)=40\)

  1. Large cheese pizzas cost $5 each, and large one-topping pizzas cost $6 each.
    Choose the equation that represents the total cost, \(T\), of \(c\) large cheese pizzas and \(d\) large one-topping pizzas.
  • \(T=6c+5d\)
  • \(T=5c+6d\)
  • \(T=5c-6d\)
  • \(T=6c-5d\)

Answer: \( T=5c+6d \)

Our equation in words would be that Total cost is going to equal (the cost of one cheese pizza times the number of cheese pizzas purchased) plus (the cost of one one-topping pizza times the number of one-topping pizzas purchased).

Substitute in the variables and prices that are given in the problem.
\(T=5c+6d\)
