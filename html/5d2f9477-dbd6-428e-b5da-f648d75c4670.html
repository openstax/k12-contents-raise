Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

For questions 1 - 4, use the following scenario.

The relationship between the amount of time a car is parked, in hours, and the cost of parking, in dollars, can be described with a function.
  1. Identify the independent variable in this function.

    Answer: The independent variable is the amount of time the car is parked.

  2. Identify the dependent variable in this function.

    Answer: The dependent variable is the cost of parking.

  3. Describe the function with a sentence of the form “____ is a function of ____.”

    Answer: The cost of parking is a function of the amount of time a car is parked.
