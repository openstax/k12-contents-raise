Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson. In questions 1-4, determine if the following points are solutions to the given system of equations.

  1. Determine if the point is a solution to the system of equations: \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}2x-6y=0\\3x-4y=5\end{array}\right.\)
  1. Determine if the point is a solution to the system of equations: \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}x-6y=0\\3x-4y=5\end{array}\right.\)
  1. Determine if the point is a solution to the system of equations: \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}x + y = 2\\y = \frac{3}{4}x\end{array}\right.\)
  1. Determine if the point is a solution to the system of equations: \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}x + y = 2\\y = \frac{3}{4}x\end{array}\right.\)
  1. Solve the system of equations by graphing: \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}3x + y = -3\\2x + 3y = 5\end{array}\right.\)
  1. Solve the system of equations by graphing: \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}y = x + 2\\y = -2x + 2\end{array}\right.\)
  1. Solve the system of equations by graphing: \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}y = \frac{3}{2}x + 1\\y = -\frac{1}{2}x + 5\end{array}\right.\)
  1. Solve the system of equations by graphing: \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}x + y = -4\\-x + 2y = -2\end{array}\right.\)
  1. Solve the system of equations by graphing: \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}-2x + 3y = 3\\x + 3y = 12\end{array}\right.\)
  1. Solve the system of equations by graphing: \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}2x - 4y = 8\\x + 3y = 9\end{array}\right.\)
  1. A bakery charges $2 per croissant and $4 per muffin. One day, they charge $42 in total and sell 15 baked goods. How many croissants and muffins did they sell?
  1. A pet store sells mice for $7 each and gerbils for $9 each. They sell 17 animals for a total of $137. How many mice and gerbils did they sell?
