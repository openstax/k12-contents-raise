  2. A seed is planted in a glass pot, and its height is measured in centimeters every day.

    The best fit line is given by the equation \(y=0.404x−5.18\), where \(y\) represents the height of the plant above ground level, and \(x\) represents the number of days since it was planted.
What is the slope of the best fit line? What does the slope of the line mean in this situation? 
    1. The slope is 5.18, which means for every day, the plant grows 5.18 centimeters. 
    2. The slope is 0.404, which means for every day, the plant grows 0.404 centimeters. 
    3. The slope is \(-5.18\), which means for every day, the plant shrinks 5.18 centimeters. 
    4. The slope is 0.404, which means the plant grows 1 centimeter every 0.404 days.
ANSWER: b

3. The scatter plots below show different lines that students used to model the relationship between body mass (in pounds) and bite force (in pounds) for crocodiles.

Match each graph to one of the equations below, and explain your reasoning. Let \(B\) represent bite force (in pounds) and \(W\) represent body mass (in pounds).



Equation 1: \(B = 3.28W +126 \)
Equation 2: \(B = 3.04W +351 \)
Equation 3: \(B = 2.16W +267 \)

ANSWER:

Line 1 and Equation 3 match because the intercept of 267 appears to match the graph, which has the second-largest intercept.

Line 2 and Equation 2 match because the intercept of Equation 2 is larger, so it matches Line 2, which has a \(y\)-intercept closer to 400.

Line 3 and Equation 1 match because the intercept of Equation 1 is the smallest, which seems to match the graph.
