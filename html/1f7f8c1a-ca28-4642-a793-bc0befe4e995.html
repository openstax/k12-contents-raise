After the activity, students will answer the following question to check their understanding of the concepts explored in the activity.

QUESTION: Which function represents \(g(x)\), a function transformed from \(f(x)\) by a horizontal compression?

Choice A \(g(x) = 5f(x)\) Choice B \(g(x) = f(5x) \) Choice C \(g(x) = f(x) + 5\) Choice D \(g(x) = f(x + 5)\)
