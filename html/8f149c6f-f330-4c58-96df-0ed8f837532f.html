  1. Select two solutions for \(x^2−2x−1.25=0\).

Correct! 2.5, -0.5

The correct answers are: 2.5, -0.5.

Select the solution button to compare your work.

Compare your answer:

Completing the square method:

\(\begin{array}{rcl} x^{2}-2 x-1.25 &=&0 \\ x^{2}-2 x &=&1.25 \\ x^{2}-2 x+\left(\frac{-2}{2}\right)^{2} &=&1.25+\left(\frac{-2}{2}\right)^{2} \\ x^{2}-2 x+1 &=&1.25+1 \\ x^{2}-2 x+1 &=&2.25 \\ (x-1)^{2} &=&2.25 \\ \sqrt{(x-1)^{2}} &=&\sqrt{2.25} \\ x-1 &=&\pm \sqrt{2.25} \\ x &=&1 \pm \sqrt{2.25} \\ x &=&1 \pm 1.5 \\ x &=&1+1.5 \\ x &=&1-1.5 \\ x &=&2.5 \\ x &=&-0.5 \\ x&=&2.5 \text { and } x=-0.5 \end{array}\)


  1. Select two solutions for \(5x^2+9x−44=0\).

Correct! 2.2, -4

The correct answers are: 2.2, -4.

Select the solution button to compare your work.

Compare your answer:

Completing the square method:

\(\begin{array}{rcl} 5 x^{2}+9 x-44 &=&0 \\ 5 x^{2}+9 x &=&44 \\ \frac{5 x^{2}+9 x}{5} &=&\frac{44}{5} \\ x^{2}+\frac{9}{5} x+\left(\frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{9}{5}\right)^{2} &=&\frac{44}{5}+\left(\frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{9}{5}\right)^{2} \\ x^{2}+\frac{9}{5} x+\frac{81}{100} &=&\frac{44}{5}+\frac{81}{100} \\ x^{2}+\frac{9}{5} x+\frac{81}{100} &=&\frac{961}{100} \\ \left(x+\frac{9}{10}\right)^{2} &=&\frac{961}{100} \\ \sqrt{\left(x+\frac{9}{10}\right)^{2}} &=&\sqrt{\frac{961}{100}} \\ x+\frac{9}{10} &=&\pm \frac{31}{10} \\ x &=&-\frac{9}{10} \pm \frac{31}{10} \\ x &=&-\frac{9}{10}+\frac{31}{10} \\ x &=&-\frac{9}{10}-\frac{31}{10} \\ x &=&\frac{22}{10}=2.2\\ x&=&-\frac{40}{10}=-4 \\ x&=&2.2 \text { and } x=-4 \end{array}\)
