In this lesson, you will learn how to work with sequences and describe them recursively in an informal way. You will see how sequences are used to accomplish recurring tasks.

When you finish this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Give examples of a sequence.
  • Explain the meaning of the term “sequence.”
  • Explain the meaning of the term “term,” as it is used in a mathematical sequence.
