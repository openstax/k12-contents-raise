Warm Up Activity

Solve each of the following proportions:

  1. \( \frac{x}{56} = \frac{7}{8} \)

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:
Cross multiply. \( 8x = 7(56) \)
Simplify. \( 8x = 392 \)
Divide both sides by 8. \( \frac{8x}{8}=\frac{392}{8} \)
Simplify. \( x=49 \)

  1. \( \frac{56}{72} = \frac{y}{9} \)

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:
Cross multiply. \( 56(9)=72y \)
Simplify. \( 504 =72y \)
Divide both sides by 72. \( \frac{504}{72}=\frac{72y}{72} \)
Simplify. \( x=7 \)

  1. \( \frac{98}{154} = \frac{-7}{p} \)
