  1. Functions \(a, b, c, d, e\), and \(f\) are given below. Which functions are linear? Select the three answers that apply.
  • \(a(x)=3x\)
  • \(b(x)=3^x\)
  • \(c(x)=x^3\)
  • \(d(x)=9+3x\)
  • \(e(x)=9 \cdot 3^x\)
  • \( f(x)=9 \cdot 3x\)

Answer:

  • \(a(x)=3x\)
  • \(d(x)=9+3x\)
  • \(f(x)=9⋅3x\)
  1. Functions \(a, b, c, d, e\), and \(f\) are given below. Which functions are exponential? Select the two answers that apply.
  • \(a(x)=3x\)
  • \(b(x)=3^x\)
  • \(c(x)=x^3\)
  • \(d(x)=9+3x\)
  • \(e(x)=9 \cdot 3^x\)
  • \( f(x)=9 \cdot 3x\)

Answer:

  • \(b(x)=3^x\)
  • \(e(x)=9 \cdot 3^x\)
  1. Here are 4 equations defining 4 different functions, \(a, b, c\), and \(d\). List them in order of increasing rate of change. That is, start with the one that grows the slowest and end with the one that grows the quickest.
    • \(a(x)=5x+3\)
    • \(b(x)=3x+5\)
    • \(c(x)=x+4\)
    • \(d(x)=1+4x\)
  • Option 1: \(a(x)\), \(b(x)\), \(c(x)\), \(d(x)\)
  • Option 2: \(d(x)\), \(c(x)\), \(b(x)\), \(a(x)\)
  • Option 3: \(a(x)\),\(d(x)\), \(b(x)\), \(a(x)\)
  • Option 4: \(c(x)\), \(b(x)\), \(d(x)\), \(a(x)\)

Answer: Option 4: \(c(x)\), \(b(x)\), \(d(x)\), \(a(x)\)

  1. The functions \(f\) and \(g\) are defined by \(f(x)=8x+33\) and \(g(x)=2 \cdot(1.2)^x\). Which function eventually grows faster, \(f\) or \(g\)? Explain how you know.
  • \(f(x)\) grows faster because 8 is greater than 1.2.
  • \(f(x)\) grows faster because 33 is greater than 2.
  • \(g(x)\) grows faster because it is a linear function.
  • \(g(x)\) grows faster because it is an exponential function.

Answer: \(g(x)\) grows faster because it is an exponential function.
