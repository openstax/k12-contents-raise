Cool Down (5 minutes)

Student-Facing Statement

A sequence starts 3, 6, . . .

  1. Give a rule the sequence could follow, and list the next 3 terms.
  1. Give a different rule the sequence could follow, and list the next 3 terms.

Student Response

  1. Sample Responses: Each term adds 3 from the previous term. The next three terms are 9, 12, 15.
  2. Sample Responses: Each term doubles the previous term. The next three terms are 12, 24, 48.
