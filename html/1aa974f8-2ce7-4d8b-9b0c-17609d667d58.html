1. When Raoul uses the treadmill at the gym, the number of calories, \( c \), he burns varies directly with the number of minutes, \( m \), he uses the treadmill. He burned 315 calories when he used the treadmill for 18 minutes. 

How many calories would he burn if he uses the treadmill for 25 minutes?
