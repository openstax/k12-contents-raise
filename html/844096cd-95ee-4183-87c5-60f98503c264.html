In this lesson, you will learn how to write terms that include rational exponents as radicals and vice versa.

When you finish this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Simplify radical expressions with even and odd indexes.
  • Rewrite expressions with rational exponents as roots.
  • Use properties for exponents with rational exponents.

Here are the activities that will help you reach those goals:

  • 5.2.1: Perfect Squares Revisited
  • 5.2.1: Roots and Radicals
  • 5.2.2: The nth Root
    • 5.2.2: Self Check
  • 5.2.3: Radicals and Rational Exponents
    • 5.2.3: Self Check
  • 5.2.4: Properties of Exponents with Rationals
