Today you are going to investigate something called an interactive simulation. It is a tool that will help you visualize how functions work.

If you do not have an activity sheet, you may use this page to complete the project.

To begin, open the Function Builder activity in your web browser. You will use it to complete this project. It will look like this:

Play with any of the screens on the Function Builder for 5 minutes.

As you discover how the tool works, share your thinking with your group members. You may want to use the following question and sentence stem to guide your discussions:

  • What did you try? What happened?
  • I tried … and it caused … to happen.

Write down two things you notice or have questions about, and one thing a neighbor noticed that is interesting to you.
