Building Character: Proactivity

Proactivity is the ability to take initiative. There’s evidence that proactivity is one of the qualities that distinguished the greatest American presidents from their peers.

Think about your current sense of proactivity. Are the following statements are true for you?

  • I am always looking for better ways of doing things.
  • I love to challenge the status quo.

Don’t worry if none of these statements are true for you. Developing this trait takes time. Your first step starts today!
