Cool Down (5 minutes)

Student Activity

  1. Draw a diagram to show that \((3x+1)(x+2)\) is equivalent to \(3x^2+7x+2\).

Write down your answer, then select the solution button to compare your work.

Answer: Diagram should show partial products of \(3x(x)\), \(6x\), \(x\), and 2, which add up to \(3x^2+7x+2\).

  1. Is \((x+4)^2\) equivalent to \(2x^2+8x+8\)? Explain or show your reasoning.

Answer: No. \((x+4)(x+4)=x^2+4x+4x+16=x^2+8x+16\).
