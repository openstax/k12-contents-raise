Reports and Measuring Progress

The following information provides guidance for how to use the components in this course to monitor and respond to student progress.


To see reports about students in this course, click on the Grades Tab at the top of the course.

From this page, all the grades students have submitted are visible.

Students have multiple attempts for quizzes and currently the highest scored quiz attempt for any quiz will appear in this page.

To see the individual question answers for quiz attempts, click on the quiz name in the report, or navigate to the quiz itself and click the Results Tab.

When looking at quiz reports, several options are available. Adjustments can be made for what to include in the report and how the information is displayed. Use the arrow icons to collapse and hide the setting features for the different options. The quiz attempts and individual quiz scores are listed below this section.



If a student attempted a quiz multiple times, the score displays in blue. All of the quiz attempts for the students are also visible here.

When Review Attempt is selected, teachers can access which answer choice the student selected as well as the text of the question.


More detailed information on the formal assessment structure of the course as well as the standards blueprint for all assessments can be accessed in the Course Design section about Assessments in the Appendix.
