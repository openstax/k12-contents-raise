Student Activity

A quadratic function is defined by the expression \(x^2−20x+19\).

Rewrite the expression in vertex form (of a quadratic expression) and give the coordinates of the vertex (of a graph). Show your reasoning.

Write down your answer, then select the solution button to compare your work.

Answer: \((x−10)^2−81\). The vertex is at \((10, -81)\).
