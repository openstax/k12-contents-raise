1. Solve

\( \left\{ \begin{array}{c l} –2x + 5y = –42\\ 7x + 2y = 30 \end{array}\right. \)

  • \((0,15)\)
  • \((11,−4)\)
  • \((1,−8)\)
  • \((6,−6)\)

Answer: \( (6,-6)\)

\(- 2( –2x+5y=–42)=4x-10y=84 \)

\(5(7x+2y=30)=35x+10y=150\)

\(\begin{array}{r}4x-10y&=&84 \\35x+10y&=&150 \\ \hline 39x&=&234 \\x&=&6 \end{array}\)

\(\begin{array}{rcl}7(6)+2y&=&30 \\42+2y&=&30 \\2y&=&-12 \\y&=&-6 \\\end{array}\)

The solution is \((6,-6)\).


2. Solve

  • \((2,0)\)
  • \((1,8)\)
  • \((−4,2)\)
  • \((−9,−4)\)

The solution is \((-4,2)\).

6. Solve

\( \left\{ \begin{array}{c l} \frac{3}{2}x - \frac{1}{4}y = 5\\ \frac{1}{3}x + \frac{1}{6}y = 2 \end{array}\right. \)

  • \((4,4)\)
  • \((1,-5)\)
  • \( (-\frac{1}{5}, \frac{2}{3})\)
  • \((-4,-5)\)

Answer: (4,4)

\( 4( \frac32 x-\frac14 y=5)=6x-y=20\)

\(6( \frac13 x+\frac16 y=2)=2x+y=12\)

\(\begin{array}{r} 6x-y&=&20 \\ 2x+y&=&12 \\ \hline 8x&=&32 \\x&=&4 \end{array}\)

\(\begin{align*} 2(4) + y &= 12 \\ 8 + y &= 12 \\ y &= 4 \\ \end{align*}\)

The solution is \((4,4)\).


7.  Solve  

\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}\frac13x\;+\;\frac19y\;=\;2\\–\;\frac12x\;+\;\frac45y\;=\;–3\end{array}\right.\)

  • \((6,0)\)
  • \((7,−3)\)
  • \((8,−5)\)
  • \((−2,−5)\)

Answer:\( (6,0)\)

\(9( \frac13 x+ \frac19 y=2)=3x+y=18 \)

\(10( –\frac12 x+ \frac45 y=–3)=-5x+8y=-30 \)

\(-8( 3x+y=18)=-24x-8y=-144 \)

\(\begin{align*}-5x+8y&=-30 \\-24x-8y&=-144 \\ \hline -29x&=-174 \\x&=6 \\\end{align*}\)

\(\begin{align*}3x+y&=18 \\3(6)+y&=18 \\18+y&=18 \\y&=0 \\\end{align*}\)

The solution is \((6,0)\).


8. Solve

  \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}6x\;–\;8y\;=\;\frac{16}5\\3x\;+\;2y\;=\;\frac{14}5\end{array}\right.\)

  • \((4,−4.6)\)
  • \((0,−0.4)\)
  • \((0.8,0.2)\)
  • \((2,1.1)\)

Answer: \((0.8,0.2)\)

\( 4( 3x+2y=\frac{14}{5})=12x+8y=\frac{56}{5} \)

\(\begin{align*}6x-8y&=\frac{16}{5} \\12x+8y&=\frac{56}{5} \\ \hline 18x&=\frac{72}{5} \\90x&=72 \\x&=\frac{72}{90} \\x&=\frac{4}{5} \\x&=0.8 \\\end{align*}\)

\(\begin{align*}3(\frac45)+2y&=\frac{14}{5} \\\frac{12}{5}+2y&=\frac{14}{5} \\5(\frac{12}{5}+2y&=\frac{14}{5}) \\12+10y&=14 \\10y&=2 \\y&=\frac{2}{10} \\y&=\frac15 \\y&=0.2 \\\end{align*}\)

The solution is \((0.8,0.2)\).

10.  Solve

\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}\frac12x\;+\;\frac13y\;=\;\frac13\\\frac13x\;+\;\frac14y\;=\;-\frac18\end{array}\right.\)

  • \((9,–12.5)\)
  • \((0,1)\)
  • \((6,–8)\)
  • \((12,–17)\)

Answer: \((9,–12.5) \)

\(6( \frac12 x+\frac13 y=\frac13)=3x+2y=2 \)

\(24( \frac13 x+\frac14 y=–\frac18)=8x+6y=-3 \)

\(-3(3x+2y=2)=-9x-6y=-6\)

\(\begin{align*}-9x-6y&=-6 \\8x+6y&=-3 \\ \hline -x&=-9 \\x&=9 \\\end{align*}\)

\(\begin{align*}3(9)+2y&=2 \\27+2y&=2 \\2y&=-25 \\y=&-12.5 \\\end{align*}\)

The solution is \((9,-12.5)\).
