Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

1. Solve

\( \left\{ \begin{array}{c l} –2x + 5y = –42\\ 7x + 2y = 30 \end{array}\right. \)

  1. (0,15)
  2. (11,−4)
  3. (1,−8)
  4. (6,−6)

Answer: \( x = 6 \), \( y = −6 \)

2. Solve

\( \left\{ \begin{array}{c l} 6x – 5y = –34\\ 2x + 6y = 4 \end{array}\right. \)

  1. (2,0)
  2. (1,8)
  3. (−4,2)
  4. (−9,−4)

Answer: \( x = −4 \), \( y = 2 \)

3. Solve

\( \left\{ \begin{array}{c l} 5x – y = –2.6\\ −4x − 6y = 1.4 \end{array}\right. \)

  1. (7,–4.9)
  2. (0.5,0.1)
  3. (−3,−12)
  4. (−0.5,0.1)

Answer: \( x = −1/2 \), \( y = 1/10 \)

4. Solve

\( \left\{ \begin{array}{c l} 7x – 2y = 3\\ 4x + 5y = 3.25 \end{array}\right. \)

  1. (1,2)
  2. (0.5,0.25)
  3. (–1,–5)
  4. (3,9)

Answer: \( x = 1/2 \), \( y = 1/4 \)

5. Solve

\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}5x\;+\;9y\;=\;16\\x\;+\;2y\;=\;4\end{array}\right.\)

  1. (−4,4)
  2. (3.2,0)
  3. (−8,6)
  4. (8,−2)

Answer: \( x = −4 \), \( y = 4 \)

6.  \( \left\{ \begin{array}{c l} \frac{5}{6}x + \frac{1}{4}y = 0\\ \frac{1}{8}x - \frac{1}{2}y = -\frac{43}{120} \end{array}\right. \)

  1. (0,3)
  2. (1,-5)
  3. \( (-\frac{1}{5}, \frac{2}{3})\)
  4. (-4,-5)

ANSWER: (-2,-1)

7.  Solve  

\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}\frac13x\;+\;\frac19y\;=\;2\\–\;\frac12x\;+\;\frac45y\;=\;–3\end{array}\right.\)

  1. (6,0)
  2. (7,−3)
  3. (8,−5)
  4. (−2,−5)

ANSWER: (6,0)

8. Solve

  \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}6x\;–\;8y\;=\;\frac{16}5\\3x\;+\;2y\;=\;\frac{14}5\end{array}\right.\)

  1. (4,−4.6)
  2. (0,−0.4)
  3. (0.8,0.2)
  4. (2,1.1)

ANSWER: (0.8,0.2)

9.   \(\left\{\begin{array}{l}5x\;–\;2y\;=\;2\\7x\;–\;4y\;=\;2.5\end{array}\right.\)

  1. (2.4,5)
  2. (2,4)
  3. (0,−1)
  4. (0.5,0.25)

ANSWER: (0.5,0.25)

10.  Solve

\(\left\{\begin{array}{l}\frac12x\;+\;\frac13y\;=\;\frac13\\\frac13x\;+\;\frac14y\;=\;-\frac18\end{array}\right.\)

  1. (9,–12.5)
  2. (0,1)
  3. (6,–8)
  4. (12,–17)

ANSWER: (9,–12.5)
