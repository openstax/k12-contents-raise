Students will complete the fullowing questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

In questions 1–3, find the equation of a line with given slope and \( y \)-intercept. Write the equation in slope-intercept form.

1. slope 3 and \( y \)-intercept \( ( 0,5) \)

  • \(y=5x+3\)
  • \(y=3x+0\)
  • \(y=3x+5\)
  • \(y=0x+3\)

Answer: \( y=3x+5 \)

2. slope \(−3 \) and \( y \)-intercept \( (0,−1)\)

  • \(y=-x-3\)
  • \(y=-3x-1\)
  • \(y=-x+0\)
  • \(y=0x-3\)

Answer: \( y=-3x-1 \)
