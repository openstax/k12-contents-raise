In this lesson, you will learn about the zero product property. You will use it to reason about the solutions to quadratic equations that each have a quadratic expression in factored form on one side and 0 on the other side.

When you finish this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Explain the meaning of the "zero product property."
  • Find solutions to quadratic equations when one side is a product of factors and the other side is zero.
