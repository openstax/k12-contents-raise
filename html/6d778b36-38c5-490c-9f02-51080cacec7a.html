2. If Jesse travels 150 miles, he uses 5 gallons of gas. Find the constant of variation.

Enter your answer here:

Check your answer:

Use the equation for direct variation \( y=kx \). So, \( 150=k(5) \). The constant of variation is \( k=30 \).
