Cool Down Activity

Lin’s job pays $8.25 an hour plus $10 of transportation allowance each week. She has to work at least 5 hours a week to keep the job, and she can earn up to $175 per week, including $10 to cover transportation per week.

  1. Represent this situation mathematically. If you use variables, specify what each one means.
  2. How many hours per week can Lin work? Explain your reasoning.

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answers.

  1. \(8.25h\;+\;10\;\leq\;175\) and \(h\;\geq\;5\), where \(h\) represents the number of hours Lin works in a week.
  2. At least 5 hours and at most 20 hours (or \(5\leq\;h\;\leq20\)). For example: The maximum amount she could earn, not including the transportation allowance, is $165. That amount is equal to 20 hours of work, \(\frac{165}{8.25}=20)\).
