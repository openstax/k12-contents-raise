When you click on a lesson or teacher guide you will see a right hand navigation. This shows all of the Resources in the lesson



Lessons can also be navigated through with the next and previous buttons at the bottom of the page. This will bring you up and down the lesson menu.

Want specific help?

See each unit succinctly by clicking collapse all in the top right

You can also see the same units in the left hand navigation bar

Each unit contains Lessons, each of which has a Teacher Guide associated with it

Your students will only be able to see the normal Lessons

Navigating the lessons can happen via the right navigation panel

Classroom activities are in the center of the units and have an additional resource section after them

All the Mini-Lessons are specified as Additional Resources

The teacher resources for the mini-lessons are in the specific lesson pages

Practice Problems are the second to last portion of the unit

In each unit there are quizzes by section, at the end of the units, and STAAR reviews

There are also special STAAR Fall Review and Spring Review Quizzes

STAAR Reviews are in their own units

Start students in the Lesson Overview, and work through the course using the next and previous buttons
