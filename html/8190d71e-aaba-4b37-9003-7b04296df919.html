Cool Down (5 minutes)

Graphing technology should not be used in this cool down.

Student Activity

Use this linear equation to answer questions 1 - 2.

\(\left\{\begin{array}{c}2x+\frac12y=7\\6x- \frac12y=5\end{array}\right.\)

  1. Which would be a more helpful method for solving the system?

Answer: Adding the two equations

  1. Explain or show how you know.

Answer:

Your answer may vary, but here is a sample. \(\frac12y + − \frac12y = 0\), so adding the equations would eliminate the \(y\)-variable and enable solving for \(x\).

Solve the system without graphing to answer questions 3–5.

  1. Identify the \(x\) coordinate of the solution.

Answer:

\(\frac32\) or equivalent

  1. Identify the \(y\) coordinate of the solution.

Answer: 8

  1. Explain or show your reasoning.

Answer:

Your answer may vary, but here is a sample.

\(\begin{array}{r}\underline{\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;\;2x+\frac12y=7\\+\;\;\;\;\;\;6x-\frac12y=5}\\8x=12\\x=\frac{12}8\\x=\frac32\\\end{array}\)


\(\begin{array}{r}2x+\frac12y=7\\2(\frac32)+\frac12y=7\\3+\frac12y=7\\\frac12y=4\\y=8\\\end{array}\)
