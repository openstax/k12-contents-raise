Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

For 1 - 4, add the equations in the system to find a new equation.

  1. \( \left\{ \begin{array}{c l} 6x - 5y = -1\\ -6x - 3y = -39 \end{array}\right. \)
  • \(2y = -38\)
  • \(-2y = -40\)
  • \(-8y = -40\)
  • \(-8x = -40\)

Answer:\( -8y = -40 \)

  1. \( \left\{ \begin{array}{c l} 5x - 3y = -1\\ -6x + 3y = -6 \end{array}\right. \)
  • \(x = -7\) or \(-x = 7\)
  • \(-x = -7\) or \(x = 7\)
  • \(-6x = -7\) or \(6x = 7\)
  • \(-11x = -7\) or \(11x = 7\)

Answer: \( -x = -7\) or \(x = 7\)

  1. \( \left\{ \begin{array}{c l} 4x - 3y = 3\\ -4x - 10y = 62 \end{array}\right. \)
  • \(-13y = 65\)
  • \(-8x = 65\)
  • \( -8y = 65\)
  • \(-13x = 65\)

Answer: \( -13y = 65\)

  1. \( \left\{ \begin{array}{c l} 6x + 16y = -6\\ -6x - 10y = 6 \end{array}\right. \)
  • \(6y = 0\) or \(y = 0\)
  • \(6x = 0\) or \(x = 0\)
  • \(12x = 0\) or \(x = 0\)
  • \(6y = 12\) or \(y = 6\)

Answer: \(6y = 0\) or \(y = 0\)
