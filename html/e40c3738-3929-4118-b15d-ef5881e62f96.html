Find a strategy that fits and factor completely:

  1. \(36x^2y + 15xy − 6y\)
  • \(3y(3x − 2)(4x + 1)\)
  • \(3y(3x − 2)(4x − 1)\)
  • \((3x + 2)(4x − 1)\)
  • \(3y(3x + 2)(4x − 1)\)

Answer: \(3y(3x + 2)(4x − 1)\)
