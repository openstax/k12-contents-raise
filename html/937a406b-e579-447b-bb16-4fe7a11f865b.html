Cool Down Activity (5 minutes)

Student Activity

The problems below consist of pairs of equivalent expressions: one in standard form and the other in factored form. Note that the leading coefficients on the quadratic terms are all 1.

Find the missing numbers.

  1. \(x^2\; +\) __\(x\; +\) __ and \((x + 1)(x + 17)\)

Answer: 18 and 17, respectively. The completed expression is \(x^2+18x +17\).

  1. \(x^2+9x +14\) and \((x + 2)(x\; +\) __\()\)

Answer: 7. The completed expression is \((x + 2)(x + 7)\).

  1. \(x^2−7x +10\) and \((x − 2)(x\; −\) __\()\)

Answer: 5. The completed expression is \((x − 2)(x − 5)\).

  1. \(x^2−9x +20\) and \((x\; −\) __\()(x\; −\) __\()\)

Answer: 4 and 5. The completed expression is \((x − 4)(x − 5)\).
