Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.
  1. Choose the standard form of the quadratic expression.
    1. \((x+4)(x-1)\)

      • \(x^2+3x-4\)
      • \(x^2-3x+4\)
      • \(-x^2+3x-4\)
      • \(-x^2-3x+4\)

ANSWER: \(x^2+3x-4\)

    1. \((2x-1)(3x-1)\)

      • \(6x^2-5x+1\)
      • \(6x^2+5x-1\)
      • \(-6x^2-5x+1\)
      • \(-6x^2+5x-1\)

ANSWER: \(6x^2-5x+1\)

  1. Consider the expression \(8-6x+x^2\).
    1. Is the expression in standard form?

    ANSWER: No

    1. Is the expression equivalent to \((x-4)(x-2)\)?

    ANSWER: Yes

  1. Find the the standard form of the quadratic expression \((x-2)(x+8)\). Use the standard form to answer questions a, b, and c.
    1. What is the value of \(a\) in this expression \(ax^2+bx+c\)?

    ANSWER: \(1\)

    1. What is the value of \(b\) in this expression \(ax^2+bx+c\)?

    ANSWER: \(6\)

    1. What is the value of \(c\) in this expression \(ax^2+bx+c\)?

    ANSWER: \(– 16\)
