  1. What happens to the area when you interchange the length and width? For example, compare the areas of a rectangle of length 11 meters and width 14 meters with a rectangle of length 14 meters and width 11 meters.

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

The area stays the same if the length and width are interchanged because the area is the product of the length and the width.
