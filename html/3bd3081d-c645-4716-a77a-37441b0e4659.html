When Nafy started her company, she could have used systems of equations to help develop Teranga’s snack mixes. The cost, nutritional content, and weight of the ingredients are variables that could help her create her recipes. 

As you proceed through this section, you will learn how systems of equations can be used to find unknown quantities and solve problems.

This section includes 6 lessons:

  • Writing and Graphing Systems of Equations
  • Writing Systems of Equations
  • Solving Systems by Substitution
  • Solving Systems by Elimination, Part 1
  • Solving Systems by Elimination, Part 2
  • Solving Systems by Elimination, Part 3
  • Systems of Linear Equations and Their Solutions
