Extending Your Thinking

Graph the following system of equations on a coordinate plane:

\(y=2x +5\)

\(4x-2y =10\)

Find the solution.

(Hint: For the second equation, solve for \(y\) first.)

Infinitely many solutions. When the second equation is solved for \(y\), it becomes \(-2y =-4x=10\) then \(y = 2x +5\). Both equations are the same. So, on the graph, one line is on top of the other and all solutions are possible.
