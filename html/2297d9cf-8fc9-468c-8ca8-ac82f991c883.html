  1. Estimate the average rate of change in the population in each state between 1970 and 2010. Show your reasoning.

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

The population of California was about 20 million in 1970 and 37 million in 2010.

The average rate of change is: \(\frac{37-20}{2010-1970}=\frac{17}{40}=0.425\) million per year. The population of Texas was about 11 million in 1970 and 25 million in 2010. The average rate of change is \(\frac{25-11}{2010-1970}=\frac{14}{40}=0.35\) million per year.
