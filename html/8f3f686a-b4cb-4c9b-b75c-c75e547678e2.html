This graph represents the revenue in dollars that a company expects if they sell their product for \(p\) dollars.

  1. Based on this model, which price would generate more revenue for the company, \($5\) or \($17\)?

    2. Answer: \($5\), as it would generate about \($3,700\) in revenue. Charging \($17\) would bring in about \($2,500\).

  1. At what price (in dollars) should the company sell their product if they wish to make as much revenue as possible?

    2. Answer: \(10\)

  1. How much revenue (in dollars) would they make?

    2. Answer: \(5,000\)

  1. What is an appropriate domain for the function? Explain how you know.

    2. Write down your answer, then select the solution button to compare your work.

    Answer: \(0 \leq p \leq 20\), because the price has to be non-negative, and the company would earn negative revenue if the price were more than \($20\).
