Warm Up Activity

Use the patterns you notice to complete the tables. Show your reasoning. Complete your work on paper. Then select the Solution button to compare your work.

Table A

\(x\) 0 1 2 3 4 25
\(y\) 2.5 10 17.5 25    

Write down your answer, then select the solution button to compare your work.

Compare your answers:

Table A: 32.5 when \(x\) is 4, and 190 (or \(2.5+25 \cdot 7.5\)) when \(x\) is 25.
