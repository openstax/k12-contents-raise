In this lesson, you will continue to examine and create linear models with linear equations relating two variables. You will also look at several ways to write the equation of a line based on the information given.

When you finish this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Write an equation of the line given the slope and \( y \)-intercept.
  • Write an equation of the line given the slope and a point.
  • Write an equation of the line given two points.
