Cool Down (5 minutes)

Student Facing Statement

Consider the equation \( 1.5x + 4.5y = 18 \). For each questionexplain your reasoning.

1. If we graph the equation, what is the slope of the graph?
2. Where does the graph intersect the \( y \)-axis?
3.Where does it intersect the \( x \)-axis?

Student Response

1. The slope is \( -\frac13 \). Rearranging the equation to isolate \( y \) gives \( y=- \frac13 x+4 \).
2. The graph intersects the \( y \)-axis at \( (0,4) \). The 4 in \( y=- \frac13 x+4 \) tells us where the graph crosses the \( y \)-axis.
3. The graph intersects the \( x \)-axis at \( (12,0) \). Substituting 0 for \( y \) in the original equation and solving for \( x \) gives \( x=12 \).

Response to Student Thinking

More Chances
Students will have more opportunities to understand the mathematical ideas in this cool-down in 1.12 and 1.13, so there is no need to slow down or add additional work to the next lessons. Instead, use the results of this cool-down to provide guidance for what to look for and emphasize over the next several lessons to support students in advancing their current understanding.
