In this lesson, you will learn to complete the square to solve non-monic quadratic equations, in which the squared term has a coefficient other than 1.

When you finish this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Complete the square for quadratic expressions of the form \(ax^2+bx+c\) when \(a\) is not 1 and explain the process.
  • Solve quadratic equations in which the squared term coefficient is not 1 by completing the square.
