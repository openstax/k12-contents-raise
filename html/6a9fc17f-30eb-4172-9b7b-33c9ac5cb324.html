Warm Up Activity

Consider the equation \(q = 4 + 0.8p\).

  1. What value of \(q\) would make the equation true when \(p\) is 7?

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

\(q = 4 + 0.8(7)\)

\(q = 4 + 5.6\)

\(q=9.6\)


  1. What value of \(q\) would make the equation true when \(p\) is 100?

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

\(q = 4 + 0.8(100)\)

\(q = 4 + 80\)

\(q=84\)


  1. What value of \(p\) would make the equation true when \(q\) is 12?

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

\(12 = 4 + 0.8p\)

\(12 - 4 = 4 + 0.8p - 4\)

\(8 = 0.8p\)

\( 8 0.8 = 0.8p 0.8\)

\(p=10\)
