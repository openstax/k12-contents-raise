In this unit, students will: 

  • Write and solve linear equations and inequalities given multiple representations of relations. 
  • Determine the slope of a line given multiple representations of linear equations.
  • Represent linear equations in various forms (standard, slope-intercept, point slope).

To accomplish these unit-level learning goals, students will complete the following lessons and activities.
