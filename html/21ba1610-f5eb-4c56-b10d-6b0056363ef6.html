Student Activity

Use the following polynomial functions to complete the multiplication.

\(f(x) = 3x + 2b\)
\(g(x) = 3x - 2b\)
\(h(x) = 7x + 2\)
\(j(x) = 9x^2-12x+4\)

  1. \(f(x) \cdot g(x) =\) _______

ANSWER: \(9x^2-4b^2\)


  1. \(f(x) \cdot f(x) = \) _______

ANSWER: \(9x^2-12xb+4b^2\)


  1. \(h(x) \cdot j(x) = \) _______

ANSWER: \(63x^3-66x^2+4x+8\)


  1. \(h(x) \cdot h(x) =\) _______

ANSWER: \(49x^2+28x+4\)
