Extending Your Thinking

Simplify.

\((6a^3-2a^2b)-2(ab^2+3b^3)+(2a^2b+2ab^2)\)

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

\(6a^3-6b^3\)

Here is how to solve this problem:
\((6a^3-2a^2b)-2(ab^2+3b^3)+(2a^2b+2ab^2)\)

Step 1
Distribute the multiplication over addition.
\(6a^3-2a^2b-2ab^2-6b^3+2a^2b+2ab^2\)

Step 2
Use the Commutative Property to rearrange like terms together.
\(6a^3-2a^2b+2a^2b-2ab^2+2ab^2-6b^3\)

Step 3
Combine like terms.
\(6a^3-6b^3\)
