In this lesson, you will gain understanding of the structure of perfect-square expressions. This lesson will also help you see that perfect squares can be handy for solving equations because we can find their square roots.

When you finish this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Recognize perfect-square expressions written in different forms.
  • Recognize quadratic equations that have a perfect-square expression and solve the equations.
