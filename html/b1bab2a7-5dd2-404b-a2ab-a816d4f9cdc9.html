  1. Talk to a partner. How can you prove \(\sqrt[2]{4^2}\;=\;4\)?

Write down your answer. Then select the Solution button to check your work.

Your answers may vary, but here is an example:

Since \(4^2\;=\;4\;\cdot\;4\;=\;16\) and \(\sqrt{16}\;=4\), \(\sqrt[2]{4^2}\;=\;4\).

Notice that the root and exponents are the same and simplify to leave the integer only.
