  1. The equation \( 4(x-2) = 100 \) is a true equation for a particular value of \( x \). Explain why \( 2(x-2) = 50 \) is also true for the same value of \( x \).

Answer:

Compare your answer:
If \( 4(x−2) \) and 100 are equal, then multiplying \( 4(x−2) \) by \( \frac{1}{2}\) and multiplying 100 by \( \frac{1}{2} \) will give two results that are also equal, so \( 2(x−2)=50 \) must be true.
