In this lesson, you learned how to:

  • Calculate rates of change of functions given graphs, equations, or tables.
  • Use rates of change to describe how a linear function and an exponential function change over equal intervals.

Here are the activities that helped you reach those goals:

  • 5.15.1: Writing Equivalent Expressions
    • In this activity, you practiced writing equivalent expressions using the order of operations, combining like terms, and using properties of exponents.
  • 5.15.2: Linear Function Constant Rate of Change
    • In this activity, you revisited and extended your understanding of the constant rate of change for a linear function.
  • 5.15.2: Self Check 
  • 5.15.3: Growth Rate of an Exponential Function
    • In this activity, you examined what happens to the values of an exponential function \(f\) when the input is increased by 1 and then when its value changes by a factor of \(b\).
  • 5.15.3: Self Check 
  • 5.15.4: Comparing Linear and Exponential Growth
    • In this activity, you compared linear and exponential growth.
