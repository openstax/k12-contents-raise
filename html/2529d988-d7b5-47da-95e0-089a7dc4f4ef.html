Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.
  1. Which of the following is equivalent to \(10^2−12^2\)?
  • \((10 + 12)(10 + 12)\)
  • \((10 − 12)(10 − 12)\)
  • \((10 −12)^2\)
  • \((10 + 12)(10 − 12)\)

Answer: \((10 + 12)(10 − 12)\)

For questions 2–10, select the expression in factored form.

  1. \(c^2 − 25\)
  • \(c(c − 25)\)
  • \((c − 5)(c − 5)\)
  • \((c − 5)(c + 5)\)
  • \((c + 5)(c + 5)\)

Answer: \((c − 5)(c + 5)\)

  1. \(9w^2 − 121\)
  • \((3w − 11)(3w + 11)\)
  • \((3w + 11)(3w + 11)\)
  • \(w(9w − 121)\)
  • \((3w − 11)(3w − 11)\)

Answer: \((3w − 11)(3w + 11)\)
