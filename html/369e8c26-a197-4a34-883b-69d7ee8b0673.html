An exponential function is written in the form \(f(x)=ab^x\), where \(a\) is the initial value and \(b\) is the growth factor.


  1. In a biology lab, a population of 50 bacteria reproduce by splitting. Every hour, on the hour, each bacterium splits into two bacteria.

    1. Write a sentence of the form “_____ is a function of _____.”

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

The number of bacteria is a function of time passed in hours.



  1. Indicate which is the independent and which is the dependent variable.

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

Independent: number of hours. Dependent: number of bacteria.



  1. Write an equation that represents the situation using function notation.

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

\( f(t)=50 \cdot 2^t\)


  1. Every year after a new car is purchased, it loses \(\frac13\) of its value. Let’s say that the new car costs $18,000.

    1. Write a sentence of the form “_____ is a function of _____.”

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

The value of a car is a function of the number of years since purchase.



  1. Indicate which is the independent and which is the dependent variable.

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

Independent: number of years since purchase of car. Dependent: value of car in dollars.



  1. Write an equation that represents the situation using function notation.

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

\( f(t)=18,000 \cdot (\frac{2}{3})^t\). Note: \(\frac13\) is the rate, and growth factor \(=1-\) rate, so the growth factor is \(\frac23\) because there’s \(\frac23\) left.


  1. To control an algae bloom in a lake, scientists introduce some treatment products. The day they begin treatment, the area covered by algae is 240 square yards. Each day since the treatment began, a third of the previous day’s area (in square yards) remains covered by algae. Time \(t\) is measured in days.

    1. Write a sentence of the form “_____ is a function of _____.”

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

The area covered by algae is a function of the number of days since the treatment.


  1. Indicate which is the independent and which is the dependent variable.

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

Independent: number of days since treatment. Dependent: area of algae in square yards.




  1. Write an equation that represents the situation using function notation.

Enter your answer here:

Compare your answer:

\( f(t)=240 \cdot (\frac{1}{3})^t\). Note: \(\frac13\) is given as the growth factor because it represents what remains.
