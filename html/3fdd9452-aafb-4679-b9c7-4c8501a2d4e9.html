Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

  1. \(14ab^2-8ab^2 =\) ________
    • \(6ab^2\)
    • \(22ab^2\)
    • \(14ab^2\)
    • \(8ab^2\)

ANSWER: \(6ab^2\)

  1. \(5xy^2+7xy^2 =\) ________
    • \(12xy\)
    • \( 5xy^2\)
    • \(-2xy^2\)
    • \(12xy^2\)

ANSWER: \(12xy^2\) 

  1. \((7b^2-2b-12)-(4b^2+9b-7) =\) ___________
    • \(11b^2+7b-19\)
    • \(3b^2+7b-19\)
    • \(3b^2-11b-5\)
    • \(11b^2-11b-5\)

ANSWER: \(3b^2-11b-5\)

  1. \( (7mn^3-2m^2n-12mn)-(3mn^3-4mn) =\) ___________
    • \(10mn^3-2m^2n-16mn\)
    • \(4mn^3-2m^2n-8mn\)
    • \(4mn^3-2m^2n-16mn\)
    • \(4mn^3-10m^2n\)

ANSWER: \(4mna^3-2m^2n-8mn\)

  1. \( (r^3-r^2s)-2(rs^2+s^3)+(r^2s+2rs^2) =\) ___________
    • \(r^3+2s^3\)
    • \(r^3-rs^2-2s^3\)
    • \(r^3-2s^3\)
    • \(r^3+rs^2-2s^3\)

ANSWER: \(r^3-2s^3\)
