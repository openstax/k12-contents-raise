Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

  1. Choose the expression that best describes the diagram.

    • \((2x+5)(x+3)\) is equivalent to \(2x^2+5x+8\).
    • \((2x +5)(x +3)\) is equivalent to \(2x^2+14x + 23\).
    • \((2x+5)(x+3)\) is equivalent to \(2x^2+11x+15\).
    • \((2x +5)(x +3)\) is equivalent to \(30x^2\).

ANSWER: \((2x+5)(x+3)\) is equivalent to \(2x^2+11x+15\).

  1. Select three expressions that are equivalent to \(x^2+4x\).
    • \(x(x+4)\)
    • \((x+2)^2\)
    • \((x+x)(x+4)\)
    • \((x+2)^2-4\)
    • \((x+4)x\)

ANSWER: \(x(x+4)\), \((x+2)^2-4\), \((x+4)x\)
