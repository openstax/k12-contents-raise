SUMMARY OF EXPONENT PROPERTIES

If \(a\) and \(b\) are real numbers and \(m\) and \(n\) are integers, then

Property

Description

Product Property

\(a^m \cdot a^n={a^{m+n}}\)

Power Property

\((a^m)^n=a^{m·n}\)

Product to a Power

\((ab)^m=a^mb^m\)

Quotient Property

\(\frac{a^m}{a^n}=a^{m−n}\), \(a\neq0\)

Zero Exponent Property

\(a^0=1\), \(a\neq0\)

Quotient to a Power Property

\((\frac{a}{b})^m=\frac{a^m}{b^m}\), \(b\neq0\)

Properties of Negative Exponents

\(a^{−n}=\frac{1}{a^n}\) and \(\frac{1}{a^{−n}}=a^n\)

Quotient to a Negative Exponent

\((\frac{a}{b})^{−n}=(\frac{b}{a})^n \)
