Cool Down (5 minutes)

Student Activity

Work with a partner. For each trinomial, explain which method you would use to factor. When you have discussed your reasoning, factor the trinomial and compare solutions.

  1. \( x^2 − 14x − 32\)

ANSWER: trial and error; for example, \((x − 16)(x + 2)\)

  1. \(6r^2 + 30r + 36\)

ANSWER: the “\(ac\)” method; for example, \(6(r + 3)(r + 2)\)

  1. \((x + 3)^2 − 9(x + 3) − 36\)

ANSWER: substitution; for example, \((x − 9)(x + 6)\)
