Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

In the following exercises, determine whether each ordered pair is a solution to the system.

1. \(3x + y > 5\)

     \(2x-y\leq10\)

a. \((3,−3)\) 

Answer: Yes

b.  \((7,1)\)

Answer: No

2. \(4x − y < 10\)
    \(−2x + 2y > −8\)

a.  \((5,−2)\) 

Answer: No

b. \((−1,3)\)

Answer: Yes

3.  \(y\;>\;\frac23x\;-\;5\)
      \(x\;+\;\frac12y\;\leq\;4\)

a.  \((6, −4)\) 

Answer: No

b. \((3, 0)\)

Answer: Yes

4.  \(y\;<\;\frac32x\;+\;3\)
      \(\frac34x\;-\;2y\;<\;5\)

a. \((−4,−1)\)

Answer: No

 b. \((8, 3)\)

Answer: Yes

5.  \(6x − 5y < 20\)
      \(-2x+7y>-8\)

a. \((1, −3)\) 

Answer: No

b. \((−4, 4)\)

Answer: Yes
