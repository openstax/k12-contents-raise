In this lesson, you will write, solve, and graph linear inequalities in two variables.

When you finish this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Determine which side of the line the solutions to the inequality will fall, given a two-variable inequality and the graph of the related equation.
  • Describe the graph that represents the solutions to a linear inequality in two variables.

Below are the knowledge and skills you will practice in this lesson. Before you begin, read each one and rate how much you currently know about each one. It’s okay if your ratings are low; the purpose of this is to check in with yourself about what you do or do not yet know.
