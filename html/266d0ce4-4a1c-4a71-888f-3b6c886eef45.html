Students will complete the following questions to practice the skills they have learned in this lesson.

Simplify each expression using the appropriate property from the lesson.

  1. \(f^2 \cdot f^6\)
    1. \(f^{36}\)
    2. \(f^4\)
    3. \(f^{12}\)
    4. \(f^8\)

ANSWER: d. \(f^8\)

  1. \(\frac{(-y)^6}{(-y)^3}\)
    1. \((-y)^3\)
    2. \((-y)^2\)
    3. \((-y)^9\)
    4. \((-y)^{18}\)

ANSWER:  a. \((-y)^3\)

  1. \((-4)^0\)
    1. \(0\)
    2. \(4\)
    3. \(1\)
    4. \(-4\)

ANSWER: c. \(1\)

  1. \(2^{-3}\)
    1. \( 8\)
    2. \(-6 \)
    3. \(\frac18\)
    4. \( -\frac18 \)

ANSWER: c. \(\frac18\)

  1. \(\frac{1}{(-t)^{-5}}\)
    1. \( t^4\)
    2. \(t^5\)
    3. \(\frac{1}{t^5}\)
    4. \((-t)^5\)

ANSWER: d. \((-t)^5\)

  1. \((-\frac{m}{n})^{-3}\)
    1. \(\frac{n^3}{m^3} \)
    2. \(\frac{m^3}{n^3}\)
    3. \(-\frac{n^3}{m^3}\)
    4. \( -\frac{m^3}{n^3}\)

ANSWER: c. \(-\frac{n^3}{m^3}\)

  1. \((d^3)^5\)
    1. \( d^{15}\)
    2. \(d^8\)
    3. \(d^5 \)
    4. \(d^{-2}\)

ANSWER: a. \( d^{15}\)

  1. \(( 5x^{-2})^3\)
    1. \(\frac{ 125}{x^5}\)
    2. \(5x\)
    3. \(\frac{125}{x^6}\)
    4. \(125x\)

ANSWER: c. \(\frac{125}{x^6}\)

  1. \((\frac{7b^3c}{d^2})^2\)
    1. \(\frac{d^4}{49b^6c^2}\)
    2. \(\frac{7b^6c^2}{d^4}\)
    3. \(\frac{49b^6c^2}{d^4}\)
    4. \(\frac{49b^5c^2}{d^4}\)

ANSWER: c. \(\frac{49b^6c^2}{d^4}\)

  1. \((\frac{3z^{-2}}{w^2})^{-2}\)
    1. \(9z^4w^4\)
    2. \(\frac{9w^4}{z^4}\)
    3. \(\frac{w^4}{9z^4}\)
    4. \(\frac{z^4w^4}{9}\)

ANSWER: d. \(\frac{z^4w^4}{9}\)
