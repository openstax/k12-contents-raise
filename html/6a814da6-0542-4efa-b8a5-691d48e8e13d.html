Complete the following questions to practice the skills you have learned in this lesson.

For questions 1 and 2, decide whether each statement is true or false.

  1. \(x(x−5)=0\) has only one solution.
  • True
  • False

Answer: False

  1. A solution to \(x^2−49=0\) is \(x=-7\).
  • True
  • False

Answer: True

  1. Use graphing technology to determine the number of solutions for the equation \((x-4)(x+5)=−20\). Then find any solutions.
  • No solution
  • One solution; \(x=1\)
  • Two solutions; \(x=1\) and \(x=0\)
  • Two solutions; \(x=-1\) and \(x=0\)

Answer: Two solutions; \(x=-1\) and \(x=0\)

  1. Use graphing technology to determine the number of solutions for the equation \((x+2)(x+5)=−2\). Then find any solutions.
  • No solution
  • Two solutions; \(x=-3\) and \(x=4\)
  • Two solutions; \(x=-3\) and \(x=-4\)
  • Two solutions; \(x=3\) and \(x=4\)

Answer: Two solutions; \(x=-3\) and \(x=-4\)

For questions 5–8, choose the best method to solve the equation and then use the method to find the solution.

  1. \(x(x−6)=0\)
  • \(x=0\) and \(x=-6\)
  • \(x=6\)
  • \(x=0\) and \(x=6\)
  • \(x=6\) and \(x=-6\)

Answer: \(x=0\) and \(x=6\)

  1. \(\frac {1}{2}(x−4)^2=32\)
  • \(x=-4\) and \(x=4\)
  • \(x=-12\) and \(x=4\)
  • \(x=-8\) and \(x=8\)
  • \(x=12\) and \(x=-4\)

Answer: \(x=12\) and \(x=-4\)

  1. \((x+7)(x−2)=−8\)
  • \(x=-7\) and \(x=2\)
  • \(x=-8\) and \(x=-1\)
  • \(x=-6\) and \(x=1\)
  • No solutions

Answer: \(x=-6\) and \(x=1\)

  1. \((x−4)(x+9)=−42.25\)
  • \(x=-2.5\)
  • \(x=-3\) and \(x=-2\)
  • \(x=-5\) and \(x=-1\)
  • No solutions

Answer: \(x=-2.5\)

  1. Which statement is true of the equation \(x^2+36=0\)?
  • The solutions are \(x=0\) and \(x=6\).
  • The solution is \(x=6\).
  • The solutions are \(x=6\) and \(x=-6\).
  • The equation has no solutions.

Answer: The equation has no solutions.

  1. Which statement is true of the equation \(x^2+9x=0\)?
  • The solution is \(x=-9\).
  • The solutions are \(x=0\) and \(x=-9\).
  • The solutions are \(x=9\) and \(x=-9\).
  • The equation has no solutions.

Answer: The solutions are \(x=0\) and \(x=-9\).
