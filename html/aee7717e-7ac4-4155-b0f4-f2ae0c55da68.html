Cool Down (5 minutes)


Student Activity

Use the following scenerio for questions 1 and 2.

Lin’s job pays $8.25 an hour plus $10 of transportation allowance each week. She has to work at least 5 hours a week to keep the job, and she can earn up to $175 per week, including $10 to cover transportation per week.

  1. Represent this situation mathematically. If you use variables, specify what each one means.

Answer:

Your answer may vary, but here is an example.

\(8.25h\;+\;10\;\leq\;175\) and \(h\;\geq\;5\), where \(h\) represents the number of hours Lin works in a week.

  1. How many hours per week can Lin work? Be prepared to show your reasoning.

Answer:

Your answer may vary, but here is an example.

At least 5 hours and at most 20 hours (or \(5\leq\;h\;\leq20\)). For example: The maximum amount she could earn, not including the transportation allowance, is $165. That amount is equal to 20 hours of work, \(\frac{165}{8.25}=20\)."
