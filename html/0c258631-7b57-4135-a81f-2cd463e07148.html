Narrative

In this culminating project, you will integrate the ideas from the unit and engage in multiple aspects of mathematical modeling.


In the first activity, you interpret and analyze given models that represent the constraints and conditions in a situation. In the second activity, you create your own models after specifying quantities of interest, identifying relevant information, and setting the constraints.

Project Activities

  1. Determining a Solution to Inequalities
  2. Interpret a Set of Mathematical Models
  3. Perform Mathematical Modeling

Learning Targets

When you finish this project, you will have demonstrated how to:

  • Interpret inequalities and graphs in a mathematical model.
  • Choose variables, specify the constraints, and write inequalities to create a mathematical model.
