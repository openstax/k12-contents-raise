Original System of Equations

\( \left\{ \begin{array}{c l} 12a + 5b = -15\\ 8a + b = 11\end{array}\right. \)


Step 1 - Multiply the second equation by \( \frac{3}{2} \)

\(\frac32(8a+b=11)=12a+\frac32b=\frac{33}{2}\)


Step 2 - Subtract the resulting equation from the first equation to eliminate the variable \( a \).

\(\begin{align*}12a+5b&=-15 \\-12a-\frac32b&=-\frac{33}{2} \\ \hline \frac72b&=-\frac{63}{2}\\ \end{align*}\)

Step 3 - Multiply the second equation by 2 to find the variable \(b\).

\(\begin{align*}2(\frac72b&=-\frac{63}{2}) \\7b&=-63 \\b&=-9 \end{align*}\)
