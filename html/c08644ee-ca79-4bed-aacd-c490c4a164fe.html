In this lesson, you will be introduced to quadratic expressions in vertex form and learn that this form allows us to easily see where the vertex of a graph is located.

When you finish this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Recognize the "vertex form" of a quadratic equation.
  • Relate the numbers in the vertex form of a quadratic equation to its graph.
