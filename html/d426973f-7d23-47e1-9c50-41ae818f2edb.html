Warm Up Activity

List these quantities in order, from least to greatest, without evaluating each expression. Be prepared to explain your reasoning.

  1. \(2^{10}\)
  2. \(10^2\)
  3. \(2^9\)
  4. \(9^2\)

Write down your answer, then select the solution button to compare your work.

The correct order is \(9^2\), \(10^2\), \(2^9\), \(2^{10}\). This would be 18, 100, 512, 1024.
