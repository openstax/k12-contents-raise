In this lesson, you will work to find the slope of a line from two points, a graph, and a table. You will then use this knowledge to write equations of lines in three different forms: slope-intercept form, point-slope form, and standard form.

When you finish this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Write the slope of a line.
  • Write the equation of a line in different forms.
