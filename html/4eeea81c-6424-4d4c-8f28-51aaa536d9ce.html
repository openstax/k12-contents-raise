For 1 - 4 use the scenerio below:

Priya is buying raisins and almonds to make trail mix. Almonds cost $5.20 per pound and raisins cost $2.75 per pound. Priya spent $11.70 buying almonds and raisins. The relationship between pounds of almonds, pounds of raisins, and the total cost is represented by the equation \(5.2a+2.75r=11.7\).

Approximately how many pounds of raisins did Priya buy if she bought the following amount of almonds:

  1. 2 pounds of almonds

Correct! 0.473

\(5.2(2) + 2.75r = 11.7\)
\(10.4 + 2.75r = 11.7\)
\(10.4 + 2.75r - 10.4 = 11.7 - 10.4\)
\(2.75r = 1.3\)
\((1/2.75) (2.75r) = (1/2.75) (1.3)\)
\(r = 0.473\)

Try again. Revisit activity 1.8.2.

The correct answer is 0.473.
