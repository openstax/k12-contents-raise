Tyler is not correct. For lesser values of \(x\), \(5x^2\) results in greater values than \(2^x\), but \(2^x\) eventually results in greater values because it always doubles when \(x\) increases by \(1\). When \(x=9\), \(5(9)^2=405\) and \(2^9=512\), so \(2^9\) is greater.
