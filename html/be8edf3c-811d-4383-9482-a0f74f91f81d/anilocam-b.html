Your answer may vary, but here are some samples.

  • The equation \(3a + 6=15\) can be divided by 3 on both sides and becomes \(a +2 = 5\).
  • When 6 is subtracted from both sides of the equation \(3a +6 = 15\), the equation becomes 3a = 9.
  • When the equation \(3a + 6 = 15\) has 6 subtracted from both sides and becomes \(3a = 9\), then both sides are divided by 9, the equivalent equation is \(\frac{1}{3} a = 1\).
