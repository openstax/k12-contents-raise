  1. Each expression represents the total number of dots in a pattern where \(n\) represents the step number.

Select two expressions that represent a quadratic relationship between the step number and the total number of dots. (If you get stuck, consider sketching the first few steps of each pattern as described by the expression.)

    • \(n^2\)
    • \(2n\)
    • \(n \cdot n\)
    • \(n+n\)
    • \(n+2\)
    • \(n \div 2\)

ANSWER: \(n^2\) and \(n \cdot n\)
