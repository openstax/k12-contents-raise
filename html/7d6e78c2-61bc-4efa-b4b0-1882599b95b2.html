One technique for solving quadratic equations is called completing the square. Here are two examples of how Diego and Mai completed the square to solve the same equation.

Diego:

\(\begin{aligned} x^{2}+10 x+9 &=0 \\ x^{2}+10 x &=-9 \\ x^{2}+10 x+25 &=-9+25 \\ x^{2}+10 x+25 &=16 \\ (x+5)^{2} &=16 \\ \sqrt{(x+5)^{2}} &=\sqrt{16} \\ x+5 &=\pm \;4 \\ \\ x+5=4 \text { or } x+5 &=-4 \\ x=-1 \text { and } x=-9 & \end{aligned}\)


Mai:

\(\begin{aligned} x^{2}+10 x+9 &=0 \\ x^{2}+10 x+9+16 &=16 \\ x^{2}+10 x+25 &=16 \\ (x+5)^{2} &=16 \\ \sqrt{(x+5)^{2}} &=\sqrt{16} \\ (x+5) &=\pm \;4 \\ \\ x+5=4 \text { and } x+5=-4 \\ x=-1 \text { or } x=-9 \end{aligned} \)
