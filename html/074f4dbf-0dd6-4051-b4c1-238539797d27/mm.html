Cool Down Activity

Everything that you learn in mathematics classes is connected, even if those connections are immediately obvious. Can you think of how concepts from this lesson connect with concepts that you have previously learned?

The problems below consist of pairs of equivalent expressions: one in standard form and the other in factored form. Note that the leading coefficients on the quadratic terms are all 1.

Find the missing numbers.

  1. \(x^2 \;+\) __\(x\;+\) __ and \((x + 1)(x + 17)\)

Enter the missing numbers.

Compare your answer:

18 and 17, respectively. The completed expression is \(x^2+18x +17\).
