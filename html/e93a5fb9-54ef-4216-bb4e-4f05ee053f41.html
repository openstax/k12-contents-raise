In this lesson, you will learn how a function can be represented by two different equations. You will find any term of an arithmetic sequence directly, by using the nth term formula to find any term number \(n\).

When you finish this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Describe the nth term.
  • Find terms of sequences directly.
  • Explain why different equations can represent the same sequence.
