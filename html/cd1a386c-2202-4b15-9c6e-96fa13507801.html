Mr. Das has been interested in how batteries work and how to make them last longer since he was a boy. Now he is using that interest as a PhD candidate at MIT to produce longer-lasting batteries, which can cut down the impact on the environment. He can use functions to help him analyze energy inputs and outputs as he studies battery power and efficiency.
