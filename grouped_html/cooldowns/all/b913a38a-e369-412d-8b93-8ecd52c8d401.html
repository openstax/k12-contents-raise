Why Should I Care?

Ozzie Gomez and Juan Casava own a store called Plant Shop Chicago. They use polynomials everyday to make sure the operations of their small business go smoothly.

One of the most important parts of running a business is understanding where your money is going. You need to know how much you are spending and how much you are making. For example, Ozzie Gomez and Juan Cassava can use polynomials to calculate how many different plants they can buy and how much money they can make off of each sale.
